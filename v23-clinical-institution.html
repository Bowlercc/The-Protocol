Response Categories

Four calibrated response types for every documented specimen.

Each response has been developed to address different social contexts, relationship dynamics, and personal energy levels.

TYPE 01

The Diplomat

For preserving relationships you still need. Graceful deflection with minimal collateral damage.

"Thank you for thinking of us. We're navigating this the best we can."
TYPE 02

The Educator

For when facts need stating. Informative without being condescending.

"Infertility is a medical condition. Relaxation doesn't resolve blocked fallopian tubes."
TYPE 03

The Absurdist

For matching the energy. When the comment is so absurd, only absurdity will do.

"You're right. I'll ask my ovaries to meditate and visualize success."
TYPE 04

The Realist

For when you're done. Direct, honest, and unapologetic about boundaries.

"This isn't something I'm able to discuss casually. Let's change the subject."
