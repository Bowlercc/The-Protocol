THE PROBLEM

They mean well.
It still sucks.

People say things. They think they're helping. They're not.

You've heard them at family dinners, in break rooms, from strangers who think your reproductive choices are public property.

In the moment, you freeze. Smile. Say "thank you" while your soul exits your body.

You're not overreacting. These comments hurt because they dismiss something real. And you deserve better words than the ones stuck in your throat.

"Just relax and it'll happen."You're causing this.
"Why don't you just adopt?"There's an easy fix you're ignoring.
